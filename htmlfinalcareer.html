Job description

Culinary arts major is usually a basic chef job. They work in restaurants or bakeries they make the wonderful food that people come from all over the world to try. It is a very fulfilling job because people love food and it is essential to life. It is what brings other cultures together. They make and prepare the food.

Salary

  • Median pay (annually): $42,350

  • Median pay (hourly): $19.95

Relation to ECS

This profession is not related to ECS in any way unless you have restaurant and want your own website for it, then it would relate to ECS in a way.
